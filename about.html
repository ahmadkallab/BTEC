About Kallab's Bakery

Welcome to Kallab's Bakery, where the art of baking meets a legacy of passion. Established in 1999, our bakery has been a haven for those who appreciate the finest in pastries and bread.

History of our bakery

In a quaint town, Kallab's Bakery, founded by passionate baker ahmad Kallab, has been a local gem for decades. Known for crafting delightful pastries and bread, the bakery's commitment to quality has made it a community favorite. Today, it remains a cherished spot where the legacy of Amelia's artistry lives on, creating moments of joy in every bite.
